Why this project

Problem in mining blocks

  • High burden of respiratory disease and trauma.
  • Diagnostics are distant and costly for rural families.
  • No time‑bound emergency logistics to villages.
  • Rehabilitation options are scarce and unaffordable.

Direct, practical solution

  • Mobile Diagnostics Van with ABDM‑ready records.
  • Drone medicine delivery + Community First Responders (AEDs).
  • 3D‑printed prosthetics and physiotherapy hub.
  • Real‑time dashboards for Civil Surgeon & DC.

Phased rollout (upgrade‑ready)

Phase 1 · 0–6 months

Diagnostics + Digital Backbone

  • Van with portable X‑ray, USG, ECG, spirometry, lab analyzers.
  • Cold chain; telemedicine kit; ABDM‑compliant software.
  • Team: Nurse, Lab Technician, Driver.

Outcome: Early detection at the doorstep. Digital records for continuity of care.

Phase 2 · 6–15 months

Emergency Response · Drones + CFR

  • Two DGCA‑approved medical drones; lockers; trained pilots.
  • 500+ CFRs trained in CPR; 10 AEDs installed in public spaces.
  • VLOS to start; BVLOS on approval; insurance and safety SOPs.

Outcome: Faster golden‑hour response; assured last‑mile delivery.

Phase 3 · 15–21 months

Rehabilitation & Ortho Hub

  • 3D printers (FDM + resin), scanner, CAD; clinic + physio unit.
  • Tele‑ortho consults; follow‑up care.
  • Team: Prosthetist, Physiotherapist, Technicians.

Outcome: Mobility restored; livelihoods protected.

About Dakshayani Enterprises

Pioneering Jharkhand's transition to a sustainable, solar-powered future.

OUR STORY

Empowering Ranchi, One Rooftop at a Time

Founded in Ranchi with a vision to make clean energy affordable and accessible, Dakshayani Enterprises has grown into one of Jharkhand's most respected solar EPC firms. We believe that solar power is not just an alternative, but the future of energy for our homes, businesses, and communities.

Our commitment is to provide our clients with the highest quality solar solutions, backed by transparent processes, engineering excellence, and dedicated customer support. We handle every aspect of your solar journey, from the initial consultation and subsidy paperwork to the final installation and commissioning.

Our Core Principles

These values guide every project we undertake and every customer we serve.

Our Mission

To accelerate the adoption of solar energy in Jharkhand by delivering reliable, high-performance, and cost-effective solar power systems for all.

Quality & Safety

Customer Focus

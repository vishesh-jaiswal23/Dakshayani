Shipping & Delivery Policy

This policy outlines the process for the shipping and delivery of components for your solar power system installation by Dakshayani Enterprises.

1. Scope

This policy applies to all solar components, including panels, inverters, mounting structures, and other materials required for projects contracted with Dakshayani Enterprises within the state of Jharkhand.

2. Delivery Process

Once your project is finalized and payment terms are met, our logistics team will schedule the delivery of all necessary components to your installation site. You will be notified at least 48 hours prior to the delivery date. Our team will coordinate with you to ensure a smooth offloading process.

3. Inspection on Delivery

It is the client's responsibility, or that of their designated representative, to be present at the time of delivery to inspect the components for any visible damage. Any discrepancies or damage must be reported to the Dakshayani Enterprises delivery coordinator immediately.

Compliance and Certifications

Dakshayani Enterprises is committed to the highest standards of quality, safety, and regulatory compliance.

1. MNRE Compliance

We are a registered channel partner of the Ministry of New and Renewable Energy (MNRE), Government of India. All our projects and components comply with the standards and guidelines set forth by the MNRE.

2. ISO Certification

Our company is ISO 9001:2015 certified for quality management systems, ensuring that our processes from design to installation meet international standards.

3. Component Certification

All solar panels and inverters we use are certified by relevant national and international bodies such as IEC (International Electrotechnical Commission) and BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards).

Warranty Information

Your investment in a solar power system from Dakshayani Enterprises is protected by robust, multi-layered warranties.

1. Solar Panel Warranty

Our Tier-1 solar panels come with a 10-12 year product warranty and a 25-year linear performance warranty, ensuring they will produce at least 80% of their rated power after 25 years.

2. Inverter Warranty

The inverters we install are covered by a standard manufacturer's warranty of 5-10 years, depending on the brand and model.

3. Workmanship Warranty

Dakshayani Enterprises provides a comprehensive 5-year warranty on all installation workmanship. This covers any defects arising from the installation process, including wiring and mounting.

Return and Refund Policy

This policy details the terms related to returns and refunds for services and products provided by Dakshayani Enterprises.

1. Order Cancellation & Refund

A customer can cancel their order within 7 days of signing the agreement and paying the advance. A processing fee may be deducted from the refund amount. No refund is applicable after the components have been procured for the specific project.

2. Returns

As solar projects are custom-designed and installed, the return of installed components is not possible. In the case of a manufacturing defect discovered prior to installation, the specific component will be replaced as per the manufacturer's warranty policy.
